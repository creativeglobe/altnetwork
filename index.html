Social Media Network

An alternative user-focused social network
fostering communication and diverse perspectives.

Today's social media platforms have become increasingly restrictive.

Algorithms, arbitrary rules, content moderation practices, and the extensive use of personal data have distanced many people from what social networking was originally meant to be: free expression and meaningful communication.

Our idea is simple:
to create a social networking platform open to different viewpoints, without political or thematic bias, and with genuine respect for the user.

We are not aiming to replace everything.
We aim to build a real alternative, shaped by the participation of its community.

How the project will start

At this stage, we are not asking for money.
First, we want to see whether there is real interest.
For practical reasons, during the initial phase, the platform will be available only in countries where active interest and participation are demonstrated.
This allows us to launch in a more structured way, with real users rather than artificial or unfocused exposure.

If a sufficient number of interested people come forward:
• the creation of the platform will be officially announced
• optional financial support will then be opened
• those who expressed interest early will have a voice in shaping the project

Our initial goal is to reach 10,000 people. If this goal is reached, these 10,000 early supporters will form the financial foundation that makes the project possible, supporting the development and long-term sustainability of the platform while keeping it independent and community-driven.

The platform will move forward only if there is a community that truly wants it.
